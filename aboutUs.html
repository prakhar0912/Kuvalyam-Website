About Us

Sumit Ahuja

Director

It all began as a site supervisor as it does with all great ideas and things, were following orders, and working on commands and there I discovered a lapse in the industry. My interest grew from day 1 and people gradually started to understand my curiosity for designs. I learned Architectural CAD and gained practical experience simultaneously. Meanwhile, The whole journey went from, following somebody to myself and my design aesthetics. I always carried the workmanship as my back which I earned throughout my journey as a site supervisor. I took the work as a hobby with a vision to have a home where I create a reflection of the client where they can feel the warmth in tailored-made homes for them only and not just an ordinary readymade home.
